The Mysterious Face on Mars

The image sparked excitement and speculation among scientists and the general public, igniting a desire to uncover the secrets of this enigmatic feature.

Instead, it is a landform created by natural processes on the planet's surface. The face is about 2.5 kilometers wide, which is about the size of eighteen football fields.
